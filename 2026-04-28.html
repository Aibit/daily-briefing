🤝 M&A

Creator Economy Roll-Ups Have Arrived: Fixated Acquires Studio71 in Landmark Deal

LA-based Fixated acquired Studio71 — the creator network with ~$290M in FY2025 revenue — from German media giant ProSiebenSat.1. This is Fixated's fifth acquisition in five months (also: Camp Talent, Moondust Management, Ellify, Elevate), backed by a $50M strategic investment from Eldridge Industries. The combined entity now manages 1,000+ creators with billions of monthly views across YouTube, TikTok, Snap, and Twitch.

Why it matters: The private equity roll-up playbook has landed squarely in the creator economy. Fixated's velocity signals that scale via acquisition — not organic growth alone — is now the dominant competitive strategy. Infrastructure businesses and large talent networks are the prime targets.
